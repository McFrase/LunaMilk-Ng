    Skimmed Milk Powder & Vegetable Fat

    The skimmed milk and Vegetable Fat used in the production of Luna Popular Instant Milk respectively serves the following functions -

    • The Skimmed milk powder is nutrient dense as it provides a large dose of Vitamins and Minerals with few calories.
    • The healthy mono unsaturated and poly unsaturated fatty acids in the Luna Popular Instant Milk is of great health benefit as these are easily digested and absorbed into the system without any risk of cholesterol formation as metabolism by product.
    • Improve fat-soluble Vitamin A, D, E & K absorption that are not water-soluble.
    • Protect body organs.
    • Keeps us warm.
