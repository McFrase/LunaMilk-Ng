    MAGIC WITH LUNA

    Luna has become a household name and a distinguished brand that offers everyone access to pure, healthy and nourishing milk. Children have grown to love Luna, so have women and the whole family.

    Our full cream milk is made from pure cows’ milk and contains 28 vitamins and minerals. Our filled milk option contains skimmed milk, vegetable fat, with essential milk vitamins (A, D3, B1, B2, B3, B6) and minerals required for strong and healthy growth.
