    Chocolate gateau

    Chocolate gateau is a cake flavored, With melted chocolate, cocoa powder, Or both.

    1. 225kg/8oz plain flour
    2. 350g/121/2 oz caster sugar
    3. 85g/3oz cocoa powder
    4. 1.5 tsp baking powder
    5. 1.5 tsp bicarbonate of soda
    6. 2 free range eggs
    7. 125ml/ 41/2fl oz vegetable oil
    8. 2 tsp vanilla extract
    9. 250ml/9fl oz boiling water
    10. 250ml/9fl oz Luna full cream evaporated milk
    For the chocolate icing
    1. 200g/7oz plain chocolate
    2. 200ml/7fl oz double cream

    2. 3/4 cup Popular Filled Evaporated Milk
    3. 1/2 cup water
    4. 1 cup flour
    5. 3 table spoons melted butter
    6. Butter, for coating the pan
    Toppings
    1. Cream cheese/ whipped cream
    2. Cinnamon sugar
    3. Peaches
    4. Blueberries / Strawberries
    5. Hazelnut spread and Bananas
    Café au lait

    A café au lait is a drink of strong drip brewed or French pressed coffee, to which steamed milk is added.

    1. Coffee powder
    2. 1 warm cup of Luna Filled Creamer Milk
    Types of café au lait
    1. Lattes are made with 2-3 shots of espresso and warmed milk, not brewed coffee.
    2. Cappuccinos are like lattes, except the majority of milk has been foamed, not just warmed.
    3. Macchiatos are shots of espresso with a dollop of foamed milk on top.
    Creamy Mashed Potatoes

    Mashed potatoes, colloquially known as mash, is a dish prepared by mashing boiled, peeled or unpeeled potatoes.

    1. 4lbs (12 medium) russet potatoes, peeled
    2. 1.25 cups Luna Filled Creamer Milk
    3. 2 sticks(1 cup) unsalted butter at room temperature(not melted)
    4. 1.5 tsp sea salt, or to taste
    5. 1 tbsp. fresh parsley or chives, finely chopped for garnish
    6. Pepper to taste
    7. 3 cloves garlic
    Akamu, Ogi or Pap

    Akamu (Igbo), Ogi (Yoruba) or pap is a Nigerian corn meal made from wet corn starch. It is processed from dry white or yellow corn.

    1. Wet corn starch (Akamu, Ogi or Pap)
    2. Water ( Hot and Cold)
    3. Luna Filled Creamer Milk
    4. Sugar ( to taste)
